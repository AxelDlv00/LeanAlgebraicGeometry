**Iter-118 finding (correctness fix needed):** the iff form of (3)
is **mathematically false**. The converse direction (locally free
of rank `n` plus locally of finite presentation вЯє smooth of
relative dimension `n`) requires deformation-theoretic input
(`Subsingleton (Algebra.H1Cotangent A B)` on each affine chart),
which is NOT implied by the stated hypotheses.

Counterexample: `f : Spec k вЖТ Spec k[t]` induced by the surjection
`k[t] вЖТ k`, `t вЖ¶ 0`. This `f` is locally of finite presentation;
the unique affine open of `Spec k` is `Spec k` itself; `ќ©_{k/k[t]} = 0`
(the kernel of the trivial derivation from a quotient ring is the
full module of relations), which is `Module.Free` of rank 0; yet
`f` is a closed immersion of a non-open point, hence not flat and
not smooth, so `SmoothOfRelativeDimension 0 f` is false. The iff's
`вЖР` direction therefore fails.

The corrected statement keeps the **forward direction only**
(`SmoothOfRelativeDimension n f вЖТ вИА x, вИГ U affine, x вИИ U, ќ©(U) free
of rank n`). The forward direction is the standard Jacobian-criterion
content, provable from existing Mathlib infrastructure.
