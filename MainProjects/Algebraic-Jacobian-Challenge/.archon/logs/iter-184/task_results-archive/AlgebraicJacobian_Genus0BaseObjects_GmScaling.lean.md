## `gmScalingP1_chart_agreement_cross01` (line 382)

### Attempt 1 — Recipe 2 verbatim (`cancel_epi` + simp on iso projections)

- **Approach**: `apply (cancel_epi (gmScalingP1_cover_intersection_X_iso kbar).inv).mp`
  to lift the goal from `pullback ((cover).f 0) ((cover).f 1) ⟶ Proj 𝒜`
  to `Spec ((Away X_0X_1) ⊗ GmRing) ⟶ Proj 𝒜`. Then attempt
  `simp only [gmScalingP1_cover_intersection_X_iso, Iso.trans_inv,
  Iso.symm_inv, asIso_inv, Category.assoc,
  pullbackRightPullbackFstIso_inv_fst{_assoc},
  pullbackSymmetry_inv_comp_fst{_assoc}, ...]` to collapse each iso
  step's `.inv ≫ pullback.fst/snd` projection.
- **Result**: FAILED — simp lemmas don't fire after the unfold. None of
  `pullbackRightPullbackFstIso_inv_fst`,
  `pullbackRightPullbackFstIso_inv_fst_assoc`,
  `pullbackSymmetry_inv_comp_fst`, or
  `pullbackSymmetry_inv_comp_fst_assoc` matched; all flagged "unused
  simp argument" by the simp linter.
- **Root cause** (recorded for iter-184 mathlib-idiom consult):
  the `Iso.trans_inv` rewrite produces nested `.inv` compositions
  where the outer iso step is `asIso (pullback.map ...).inv` instead
  of a directly-handled iso. The chain of 10 `≪≫` steps generates
  this `asIso`-wrapped `pullback.map` at step 4 (the `pullbackAwayιIso`
  application), and Mathlib's simp lemmas for `pullback.map` are
  named differently than the natural `pullback.map_fst/snd` (which
  do not resolve as constants).
